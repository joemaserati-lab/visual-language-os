Pattern 001 / Experimental

Progressive Recognition

A motion pattern where content moves from abstraction to readability. The user first perceives matter, then shape, then word, then meaning.

Pattern Anatomy

Noise becomes language.

State 01Noise.

Redaction 100 is used as texture or mass. It signals that information exists before it is readable.

State 02Recognition.

Redaction 70 and 50 introduce letterform structure without resolving the whole word too early.

State 03Reading.

Redaction 35 is the final state. The pattern fails if the readable state arrives too late.

Sequence Specimen

100 to 35.

Live Demo

Scroll transformation.

Individual characters change with a pseudo-random stagger, not all at once. The word must be readable before it reaches the center of attention.

System

Usage decision

Use for

Hero words and section transitions.

Large words with enough spatial presence can transform without damaging comprehension.

Avoid for

Navigation, buttons and body copy.

Functional text must be readable immediately. Do not make the interface wait for meaning.
