Progressive Recognition

A motion pattern where content moves from abstraction to readability. The user first perceives matter, then shape, then word, then meaning.

Intent

Progressive Recognition converts illegibility into a controlled reveal. It works when the transformation itself reinforces the concept of emergence, process or discovery.

SequenceNoise → Recognition → Interpretation → Reading

For Redaction: 100 → 70 → 50 → 35. The final state must always be readable.

TimingEnd before the focal zone.

The readable state should be reached before the element crosses the central visual zone of the viewport.

VisibilityNo opacity masking.

Opacity reduction weakens the perception of font-state transformation. Keep the text visible during the reveal.

Live demo

Scroll this page. Individual characters change with a pseudo-random stagger, not all at once.

System
Works for

Hero statements

Large editorial words with enough space to transform.

Risk

Small UI

Do not use it for navigation, buttons or body copy.

Fallback

Reduced motion

Render directly in Redaction 35 or Geist.
