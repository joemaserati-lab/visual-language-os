System / Tokens

Tokens make decisions portable.

Design OS tokens define the stable visual vocabulary for color, rhythm, scale and font roles. Use them as semantic decisions, not as arbitrary values.

Color tokens

Color supports hierarchy.

--ink#14110fPrimary text and high-contrast marks.
--muted#6f675fSecondary copy, metadata and quiet labels.
--linergba(20,17,15,.14)Rules, dividers and low-noise borders.
--card#fffaf1Raised content cards and modular surfaces.
--paper#fbf6ecDocumentation tables and quiet panels.
--success#255c38Positive decisions and recommended usage.
--risk#8a2f22Warnings, anti-patterns and risky choices.
