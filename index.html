Design Operating System

A decision framework for contemporary visual design: typography, hierarchy, composition, motion and production. Not a style guide. Not a template. A system for making better design decisions.

Core thesis

Design quality must be traceable.

Every visual decision should be connected to a purpose, a perceptual principle or a validated pattern. Taste is allowed, but it must not be the only argument.

Law candidateHierarchy must exist before color.

If a page only works because of color, the structure is weak. Typography, spacing and contrast must carry the page first.

Pattern 001Progressive Recognition.

Content moves from visual noise to readable information. Redaction 100 and 70 are transitional states, not final states.
